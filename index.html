About Timer Increment

This is a time incremental game where players buy upgrades and complete challenges to accelerate time accumulation, experiencing growth from slow to explosive.

Author: 陈风就是浪(QQ 399095982)

Tips: Prioritize speed and auto minutes, then collect cycle rewards and golden time to earn minutes faster.
